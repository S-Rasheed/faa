Our Pricing Models

We offer flexible pricing models designed to suit different business needs, allowing you to choose the option that best aligns with your goals and budget.

Subscription-Based Models

Clients pay a fixed monthly/annual fee for fleet or industrial services

use cases: Vehicle monitoring systems, industrial equipment servicing contracts

Pay-As-You-Go (Usage-Based)

Payments tied to actual usage (per km, per hour, per machine cycle)

use cases: Telematics, tire leasing, or EV battery swapping

Milestone/Project-Based Payments

Payments released at defined project stages

use cases: Industrial plant set-up, workshop construction, or fleet audits

Revenue Sharing / Profit-Sharing

Shared revenue model with clients/partners

use cases: Joint operation of workshops, fleet depots, or industrial service centers.

Service-Level Agreement (SLA) Based Payments

Payments tied to performance metrics like uptime, fuel savings, or efficiency gains

Digital/Mobile Payment Integration

M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and mobile wallets for ease of transactions across Africa

use cases: Driver payments, micro-subscriptions for SMEs
