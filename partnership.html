Our Business Partnership Models

Building strong alliances that drive mutual growth and long-term success

Joint Ventures (JVs)

Partner with local or international companies to jointly run fleet or industrial operations

Case In Point

JV with OEMs for fleet maintenance centers or industrial equipment assembly

Public-Private Partnerships (PPP)

Collaborate with government agencies on transport, infrastructure, or industrial efficiency programs, youth and women empowerment programs

Case In Point

EV technician training academy, road safety initiatives, or fuel monitoring projects

Strategic Alliances

Non-equity partnerships with suppliers, OEMs, or technology providers

Case In Point

Alliance with telematics firms, tire manufacturers, or industrial automation companies

Franchise / Dealership Model

Operate as a franchise or licensed dealer of global brands in fleet, logistics, or industrial equipment

Case In Point

Tire and parts distribution, workshop chains, or battery swapping stations

Consortium Partnerships

Pool resources with multiple firms to bid for large fleet or industrial projects

Case In Point

A consortium of transport operators and equipment suppliers bidding for regional logistics contracts

Revenue-Sharing Agreements

Shared income model with partners based on utilization of fleet assets, industrial machines, or workshops

Case In Point

Battery leasing, fleet rental, or shared industrial facilities

Vendor-Managed Services (VMS)

Suppliers/partners manage spare parts, fuel, or equipment inventory directly on client sites

Case In Point

Tire and parts supplier operating inside client workshops

Technology & Data Partnerships

Co-develop digital platforms with IT firms for fleet tracking, predictive maintenance, or industrial monitoring
